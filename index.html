Serie RLC circuit

When the source voltage changes in RLC circuit the voltage between capacitor and inductor will start damping.By equation we can know that there are three posible condition.

  • Under damp

when : $(\frac{R}{2L})^2-\frac{1}{LC} >0$

  • Critical damp

when : $(\frac{R}{2L})^2-\frac{1}{LC} =0$

  • Over damp

when : $(\frac{R}{2L})^2-\frac{1}{LC} <0$

Q factor and bandwidth

When measureing the voltage from resistor ,series RLC circuit became a band-pass filter .Q factor is a value that describe the energy stored in the capacitor to the energy looses from resistor.The higher Q factor means that the amplitude in resonance frequecy will become greater (more energy is saved) and the bandwidth of band-pass filter will become smaller

Q factor and bandwidth

Q factor is determined by :

$\frac{1}{R}\sqrt{\frac{L}{C}}$

The bandwidth of series RLC circuit is determined by :

$\frac{R}{L}$
